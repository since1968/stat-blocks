Winged dire wolf

Large monstrosity, chaotic evil

Armor Class

14 (natural armor)

Hit Points

37 (5d10 + 10)

Speed

50 ft. (60 ft. flying)

Skills

Perception +4, Stealth +4

Senses

passive Perception 13

Languages

—

Challenge

2 (400 XP)

Keen Hearing and Smell.

The Winged dire wolf has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell.

Pack Tactics.

The Winged dire wolf has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the wolf's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

Actions

Bite.

Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (2d6 + 3) piercing damage. If the target is a creature it must succeed on a DC Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

The Winged dire wolf is a monstrosity bred in the pits of the Abyss.
