Master Halberdier

Medium humanoid (any race), any lawful or neutral alignment

Armor Class

17 (Half-plate)

Hit Points

58 (9d8 + 18)

Speed

30 ft.

Skills

Athletics +3, Perception +2, Survival +3. Optional: Animal Handling +2

Feats

Polearm Master, Sentinel

Senses

passive Perception 11

Languages

any one language (usually Common)

Challenge

3 (700 XP)

Martial Advantage.

The master halberdier has advantage on a melee attack against a creature if that creature is within 10 feet of an ally of the master halberdier that isn't incapacitated. The master halberdier must wield a halberd to gain this martial advantage.

Polearm Master.

When the master halberdier is wielding a halberd, other creatures provoke an opportunity attack when entering the master hablerdier's reach. The Master Hablerdier can also use a bonus action to attack with the opposite end of the halberd. This attack does 1d4 bludgeoning damage.

Sentinel.

When the master halberdier hits a creature with an opportunity attack, the creature's speed becomes zero for the rest of its turn. Creatures provoke opportunity attacks even if they take the Disengage action.

Unseat Dat Horseman.

When the master halberdier successfully hits a mounted creature with an opportunity attack, the master halberdier may use a bonus action to attempt to unseat the creature. The creature must succeed on a Strength saving throw (DC 15) or be pulled from its mount. The unseated creature takes 1d6 points of damage and is knocked prone. The master halberdier must wield a halbierd to attempt this unseating action.

Battlesystem

Regiment.

The master halberdier typically belongs to a regiment stand.

Reach.

When attacking with the halberd, the master halberdier makes a bonus attack as part of its attack action.

Actions

Multiattack.

The Master Halberdier makes two halberd attacks.

Halberd.

Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (1d10 + 3) slashing damage.

Shortsword.

Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage.

Dagger.

Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d4 + 1) piercing damage.

Handaxe.

Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 1) slashing damage.

Deriving their advantage from fighting as a unit, Master Halberdiers are professional fighers trained to use halberds in group formation and do not excel as individual fighters. Halberdiers will take up arms for pay or to protect something they believe in or value. They trained to take down cavalry and are especially adept at stopping an enemy charge.

Variant: Mounted Infantry. Halberdiers with animal handling skill may be used as mounted infantry but do not fight on horseback.
