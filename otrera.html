Otrera

Medium human, Lawful Neutral

Armor Class

14 (leather armor)

Hit Points

101 (12d8 + 39) too much?

Speed

30 ft. (60 ft. when mounted)

Skills

Animal Handling +3, Nature +4, Perception +5, Stealth +6, Survival +5.

Feats

Grappler, Mounted Combatant, Sharpshooter (dismounted)

Senses

passive Perception 16

Languages

Common

Challenge

6 (2,300 XP)

Grappler.

Otrera fights with advantage in close quarters grappling. PHB 167.

Mounted Combatant.

Otrera is a dangerous foe to face while mounted. PHB 168.

Dismounted Sharpshooter.

Otrera has mastered ranged weapons and when dismounted can make shots others find impossible. PHB 170.

Martial Advantage.

Otrera can deal an extra 7 (2d6) damage to a creature she hits with a melee weapon attack if that creature is within 5 feet of an ally that isn't incapacitated, and if that creature is a humanoid male.

Ares' Own Luck.

When she makes an ability check, saving throw, or attack roll, Otrera can add a d10 to her roll. She can do this after seeing the initial roll but before any of the roll's effects occur. She can't use this feature again until she has finished a long rest.

Bracelets of Frost Giant Strength.

Otrera wears magical bracelets which grant her a strength of 23. When not wearing the bracelets her strength is 15.

Battlesystem

Solo.

Otrera is a solo who typically joins a skirmisher stand. Lightly armored, she values speed and agility over sustained melee combat.

Commanding Presence.

When Otrera joins a stand as a commander, he has advantage on all prepare, incite, morale and rally rolls.

Actions

Multiattack.

Otrera makes two melee or two ranged weapon attacks.

Longsword.

Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8+6) slashing damage, or 8 (1d10+6) slashing damage if used with two hands.

Dagger.

Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d4 + 3) piercing damage.

Composite Bow.

Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage.

Reactions

Riposte.

When a creature misses Otrera with a melee weapon attack, she can use her reaction to make a melee weapon attack agains the creature.

Founder of the Amazons, Otrera strikes fear into the hearts of men. Beautiful and strong, she leads a mounted force that combines ranged attacks and high mobility to harass and demoralize infantry with hit and run tactics.
