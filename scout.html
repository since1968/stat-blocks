Scout—Battlesystem Playtest

Medium humanoid (any race), any lawful or neutral alignment

Armor Class

14 (leather armor)

Hit Points

34 (4d10 + 12)

Speed

40 ft.

Skills

Nature +4, Perception +5, Stealth +6, Survival +5.

Feats

Alert, Mobile, Sharpshooter

Senses

passive Perception 16

Languages

any one language (usually Common)

Challenge

1 (200 XP)

Keen Hearing and Sight.

The scout has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or sight.

Alert.

Always on the lookout for danger, the scout gains a +5 bonus to initiative and cannot be surprised while conscious. Other creatures don't gain advantage on attack rolls against the scout as a result of being hidden.

Mobile.

When the scout uses the Dash action, difficult terrain doesn't cost the scout extra movement on that turn. When the scout makes a melee attack against a creature, the scout does not provoke opportunity attacks from the creature for the rest of the turn, whether the scout hit or not.

Sharpshooter.

Attacking at long range doesn't impose disadvantage on ranged weapon attack rolls. Ranged weapon attack ignore half cover and three-quarters cover. The scout may choose to take a -5 penalty to a ranged weapon attack roll. If the attack hits, add +10 to the attack's damage.

Battlesystem

Skirmisher.

The scout belongs to a skirmisher stand. Any scout stand pressed into service as a regiment has disadvantage on all morale checks. A broken scout stand will not rally.

Small Unit Integrity.

Scouts are trained to work in units comprised of a single stand and are not subject to disadvantage on attack rolls when isolated. All other rules on isolated units apply to scouts.

Agile.

Scouts treat forest and shallow water as normal terrain for purposes of calculating speed.

Actions

Spear.

Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d6) piercing damage.

Dagger.

Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d4 + 3) piercing damage.

Light Crossbow.

Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage.

Scouts are skilled hunters and trackers who provide military reconnaissance and harassment. Unless overrun, they will not engage in melee combat.
