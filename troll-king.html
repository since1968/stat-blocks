Troll King

Huge giant, chaotic evil

Armor Class

18 (natural armor)

Hit Points

126 (11d12 + 55)

Speed

40 ft.

Saving Throws

Dex +5, Con +8

Skills

Athletics +12, Intimdation +7, Perception +4

Damage Immunities

all nonmagical ranged weapons other than heavy crossbow

Senses

darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 11

Languages

Giant

Challenge

10 (5,900 XP)

Keen Smell.

The troll king has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

Regeneration.

The troll king regains 3d12 hit points at the start of its turn. If the troll king takes acid or fire damage, this trait doesn't function at the start of the troll king's next turn. The troll king dies only if starts its turn with 0 hit points and doesn't regenerate.

Actions

Multiattack.

The troll king makes two greathammer attacks.

Greathammer.

Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 25 (3d12 + 6) bludgeoning damage. If hit, the target must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be stunned.

Rock.

Ranged Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, range 60/240 ft., one target. Hit: 28 (4d10 + 6) bludgeoning damage.

Battlesystem

Solo.

The troll king is a solo who typically joins a skirmisher stand.

Terrifying leader.

Jolly and malignant, the troll king rules by fear and force. When he joins a stand as a commander, he has advantage on all incite and morale rolls. Once his troops are broken, however, he has disadvantage on rally rolls.

Rumored to live in the Trollclaws, the troll king has never been seen by a human who lived to tell about it. His hide is so thick that he's invulnerable to any nonmagical ranged weapon less powerful than a heavy crossbow.
