Troll Archer

Large giant, chaotic evil

Armor Class

15 (natural armor)

Hit Points

84 (8d10 + 40)

Speed

30 ft.

Skills

Perception +3

Senses

darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 11

Languages

Giant

Challenge

5 (1,800 XP)

Keen Smell.

The troll archer has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

Regeneration.

The troll archer regains 10 hit points at the start of its turn. if the troll takes acid or fire damage, this trait doesn't function at the start of the troll's next turn. The troll dies only if starts its turn with 0 hit points and doesn't regenerate.

Actions

Multiattack.

The troll archer makes three melee attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws; or two ranged attacks.

Bite.

Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6 + 4) piercing damage.

Claw.

Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6 + 4) slashing damage.

Longbow.

Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage.

Battlesystem

Skirmishers.

The troll archer typically joins a skirmisher stand.

Troll archers are extremely hard to control and rarely maintain formation. They are just as happy throwing down their bows and taking a bite out of their opponents.
