Crossbow expert.

Black Widow ignores the loading property of hand crossbows and shoots her crossbows at close quarters without disadvantage. She can use a bonus action to attack with a loaded hand crossbow she is holding.

Grappler.

Black Widow fights with advantage in close quarters grappling. PHB 167.

Evasion.

When Black Widow is subject to an effect that allows her to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, she instead takes no damage if she succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if she fails.
