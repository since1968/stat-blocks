Shadow Lord

Medium monstrosity, chaotic evil

Armor Class

18 (plate)

Hit Points

146 (20d8 + 56)

Speed

40 ft. (60 ft. flying)

Saving Throws

Str +6, Dex + 7, Wis +5, Con +10

Skills

Intimdation +7, Perception +5

Damage Immunities

necrotic

Damage Resistances

fire damage, and bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical weapons

Senses

darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12

Languages

Common, Abyssal, Infernal, Elvish

Challenge

11 (7,200 XP)

Keen Smell.

The Shadow Lord has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

Regeneration.

The Shadow Lord regains 2d12 hit points at the start of its turn. If the Shadow Lord takes radiant damage, this trait doesn't function at the start of the Shadow Lord's next turn. The Shadow Lord dies only if starts its turn with 0 hit points and doesn't regenerate.

Lives in the Shadows.

The Shadow Lord is cloaked in darkness. His opponents cannot tell how much damage the Shadow Lord has taken by looking at him.

Actions

Multiattack.

The Shadow Lord makes three melee attacks.

Flame of Fire Longsword.

Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d6 + 8) slashing damage plus 3 (1d6) fire damage. If hit, the target must succeed on a DC 17 Strength or Dexterity saving throw or be incapacitated.

Rumored to live on the Isle of Mintarn, the Shadow Lord preys on the souls of adventurers and travels with a pair of winged dire wolves.
