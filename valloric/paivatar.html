Paivatar (Sentient Star)

Large celestial, neutral

Armor Class

19 (natural armor)

Hit Points

200 (16d10 + 112)

Speed

40 ft. (fly 120 ft.)

Saving Throws

Con +12, Wis +11, Cha +12

Skills

Perception +11

Damage Immunities

radiant, fire

Condition Immunities

charmed, exhaustion, frightened

Damage Resistances

bluedgoning, piercing and slashing from nonmagical weapons

Senses

truesight 120 ft., passive Perception 21

Languages

all, telepathy 120 ft.

Challenge

16 (15,000 XP)

Divine Awareness.

Paivatar knows if it hears a lie.

Innate Spellcasting.

Paivatar's innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 20, +10 to hit with spell attacks). He can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

At will: detect thoughts, detect evil and good, invisibility (self only), light.
3/day each: dispel evil and good.
1/day each: commune, control weather, plane shift, true seeing.

Magic Resistance.

Paivatar has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

Radiant Presence.

Any time Paivatar is targeted by a magic missile spell, a line spell, or a spell that requires a ranged attack roll, roll a d6. One a 1 to 5, Paivatar is unaffected. On a 6, Paivatar is unaffected and the effect is reflected back at the caster as though it originated from Paivatar, turning the caster into the target.

Legendary Actions

Paivatar can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Paivatar regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.

Searing Burst.

Paivatar emits magical, celestial energy. Each creature of its choice in a 10-foot radius must make a DC 23 Dexterity saving throw, taking 14 (4d6) fire damage plus 14 (4d6) radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Blinding Gaze (Costs 3 Actions).

Paivatar targets one creature it can see within 30 feet of it. If the target can see Paivatar, the target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be blinded until magic such as the lesser restoration spell removes the blindness.

Actions

Paivatar makes no melee attacks, preferring to rely on legendary actions or disengaging from combat entirely.

Paivatar is a fallen star who has been captured by Ice Shadows on the Island of Mintarn. He would make an ideal Great Old One patron for any warlock brave enough to free him. Paivatar has taken the appearance of an old human man, but his true nature and form are unknowable.
