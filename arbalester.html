Arbalester

Medium humanoid (any race), any lawful or neutral alignment

Armor Class

13 (leather) / 18 with pavise

Hit Points

34 (4d10 + 12)

Speed

30 ft.

Saving Throws

Dex +2 (+7 when protected by pavise)

Skills

Athletics +1, Survival +1. Optional: Animal Handling +1

Senses

passive Perception 11

Languages

any one language (usually Common)

Challenge

1 (250 XP)

Martial Advantage.

The arbalester has advantage on a ranged attack roll against a creature if at least one of the arbalester's allies is within 5 feet and isn't incapacitated.

Pavise.

The arbalester fights from behind a large, fixed shield called a pavise. The arbalester may use an action to deploy his pavise in the ground with a spike attached to the bottom. While protected by the pavise, the arbalester adds +5 to his AC and dexterity saving throws, and his movement rate is 0. The arbalester must use one action to stow the pavise on his back, and it may not be wielded in hand to hand combat.

If the arbalester is subject to an effect that allows him to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, he can use his reaction to take no damage if he succeeds on his saving throw, interposing his pavise between himself and the source of the effect.

Actions

Shortsword.

Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage.

Dagger.

Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d4 + 3) piercing damage.

Heavy Crossbow.

Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 400 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d10 + 2) piercing damage.

Battlesystem

Regiment.

The arbalester typically joins a regiment stand. When setting up the pavise, the regiment may take the defend configuration.

Arbalesters with animal handling skill may be used as mounted infantry but do not fight on horseback. Unless overrun, they do not engage in melee combat.
