Hatchibombator

Medium human, lawful neutral

Armor Class

18 (Plate)

Hit Points

104 (13d8 + 39)

Speed

30 ft.

Saving Throws

Str +6, Dex +1, Con +5, Wis +1, Cha +4.

Skills

Animal Handling +2, Athletics +3, Intimidation +5, Perception +1, Persuasion +5, Survival +3.

Feats

Great Weapon Master, Heavy Armor Master, Inspiring Leader, Sentinel

Senses

passive Perception 14

Languages

Common

Challenge

6 (2,300 XP)

Great Weapon Master.

When Hatchibombator scores a critical hit or reduces a creature to 0 hit points, he can make one melee weapon attack as a bonus action. Before he makes an attack with a heavy weapon, Hatchibombator can choose to take a -5 penalty to the attack roll and add +10 to the attack's damage.

Heavy Armor Master.

Bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage that Hatchibombator takes from nonmagical weapons is reduced by 3.

Inspiring Leader.

Hatchibombator can spend 10 minutes inspiring his companions. He can choose up to six friendly creatures within 30 feet who can see, hear, and understand him. Each creature gains 14 temporary hit points. A creature can't gain temporary hit points from this feat again until it has finished a short or long rest.

Martial Advantage.

Once per turn, Hatchibombator can deal an extra 14 (4d6) damage to a creature it hits with a melee weapon attack if that creature is within 5 feet of an ally of Hatchibombator that isn't incapacitated.

Sentinel.

When Hatchibombator hits a creature with an opportunity attack, the creature's speed becomes zero for the rest of its turn. Creatures provoke opportunity attacks even if they take the Disengage action.

Battlesystem

Solo.

Hatchibombator is a solo who typically joins a regiment stand.

Master & Commander.

When Hatchibombator joins a stand as a commander, he has advantage on all prepare, incite, morale and rally rolls.

Actions

Multiattack.

Hatchibombator makes three melee attacks.

Greatsword.

Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (2d6 + 3) slashing damage. His Greatsword strikes count as magical for purposes of overcoming resistance and immunity to nonmagical attacks and damage.

Dagger.

Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 1) piercing damage.

Reactions

Parry.

Hatchibombator adds 3 to his AC against one melee attack that would hit him. To do so, he must see the attacker and be wielding a melee weapon.

Hatchibombator leads a societates fortunae comprised of scouts, arbalesters, and halberdiers. Every man is a stakeholder in the comapny, and his business is war. When we ride forth, thinks Hatchibombator as he drains his breakfast cup of wine, we're provisioned like kings, and the country trembles before us.
